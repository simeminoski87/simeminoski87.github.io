Football Champions of Macedonia U19 2021/2022

I have played football since I know about myself.Started at very young age playing with my friends on the street,we would put rocks to be our goal and bottles to mark the out line.I really enjoyed those days. After a litle bit time my father enrolled me in a football academy.At first I was at the bottom quality among other kids but later I improved.After I finished Primary School I moved to Prilep to play for Pobeda Football Club.In Prilep I spent 4 years,and the last year was really special for me.I had an injury in a pre-seaseon match but I recovered before the season started.That was my best season as a professional football player and my team finished first meaning that we became Champions of Macedonia for players under the age of 19.
